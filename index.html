Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • Prepare Time: 10 minutes
  • Cook Time: 7 minutes/batch
  • Serving Size: 2 dozen
Ingredients Quantity Measurement
Butter 1 cup
Brown Sugar 1 cup
Sugar 3/4 cup
Eggs 2 large
Vanilla 2 tsp
Flour 2-1/2 cups
Baking Soda 1 tsp
Chocolate Chips 2 cups

Step 1:Preheat: 375째
Step 2: In a large bowl, add the melted butter, brown sugar, and white sugar. Whisk until combined.
Step 3:Add eggs and vanilla. Whisk until smooth.
Step 4:Add flour and baking soda until no dry remains.
Step 5:Add chocolate chips. Mix, Scoop, and Bake.
